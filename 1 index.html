Welcome to the Seafarer Certificate Verification Portal

This service lets you search for New Zealand seafarer certificates and credentials.

Use the “Search” tab to look up seafarers by name or certificate number. For help, visit the “Help” tab.

If you're having trouble using this service, please ensure the information you're entering is correct.
